Foundation of GENIUS Insan

GENIUS Insan is a program specially established at USIM with the aim of producing great scholars of Islamic thought based on the Quran and al-Sunnah from an early age.

About Us

The establishment of GENIUS Insan is appropriate to continue the development of Islamic knowledge and to produce great Islamic scholars in the future based on the Quran and the Sunnah.

Mission

Determined to become a vessel of intelligent students based on the appreciation of the Quran and the Sunnah

Definition

Individuals with Abqari's ability to understand, interpret, memorize and apply the Quran and al-Sunnah in life and to develop Islamic scholarly traditions and to advance science and technology.

Vision

Cultivating the Quran and al-Sunnah as the core of youthful excellence.

Objectives of Establishment

Identify

To identify smart students from all over Malaysia to be educated and mentored to become superior, holistic and balanced individuals in their worldly and professional aspects.

Provide

To provide and implement student-centered learning, experiential learning, problem-based learning and e-learning.

Prepare

Prepare students with the opportunity to participate in Compressed, Accelerated and Enrichment learning to enable them to be capable, skilled and competitive to pursue higher education as early as possible.

Offer

Offering professional services and providing professional input to the public, governmental and non-governmental organizations and interested parties by becoming a credible research and consulting center on various aspects of smart children.

Produce

Produce research, writing and publishing activities by becoming an academy that will stimulate and educate smart children produced by local, regional and global scientists and professionals.

Nurture

To nurture great Islamic scholars, thinkers, technocrats, scientists and professionals based on the Quran and the Sunnah.

GENIUS Insan is one of the national projects in producing future Islamic scholars.

It is the only such program in the Islamic world with a great module and curriculum developed by experts and scientists from the University of Islamic Sciences Malaysia (USIM). A program specially formulated with research-based formulas, will surely build excellence into Abqari individuals recognised internationally in various fields, guided by the balance of the afterlife.
